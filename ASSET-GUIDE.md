# Photo & Asset Guide — Caitlin Onie Photography

Checklist of every photo and file the site needs. You'll upload all of these through the **admin panel** at `/admin/` — no GitHub needed.

## How to upload

1. Go to `https://[your-site].netlify.app/admin/`
2. Log in with your invited email
3. Click any image field → upload from your computer
4. Decap CMS handles renaming + saving automatically
5. Click **Publish** when done — site updates in ~60 seconds

## File rules

- **All lowercase** filenames, **no spaces** (use hyphens)
- **No special characters** (no `&`, `'`, `()`, etc.)
- **Use `.jpg`** for photos, **`.svg`** for logo, **`.png`** for icons with transparency
- **Compress** before uploading — aim for under 500KB. Use [tinypng.com](https://tinypng.com) or [squoosh.app](https://squoosh.app)
